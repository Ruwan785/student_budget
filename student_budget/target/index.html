🎓 Student Monthly Finance Tracker

💡 The Problem

Students often struggle with managing income and expenses effectively. This tool simplifies personal finance tracking through an easy-to-use, question-based interface.

🔧 The Solution

This program helps students track their monthly income and spending by asking simple questions and generating a detailed summary report at the end.

📖 How to Use

The app guides you step-by-step through a series of questions:

🕒 How many hours did you work this month? 50
💶 What is your hourly wage (€)? 10
📤 Please enter the amount you spent for each category:
💰 How much did you spend on Housing (€)? 200
💰 How much did you spend on Utilities (€)? 50
💰 How much did you spend on Food (€)? 100
...and so on for all categories.
